The perfect sangria recipe

My favorite Sangria recipe was given to me by the master bartender at La Ciutat Comtal, Barcelona's most popular tapas bar, during a visit there a couple of years ago.

That recipe is special because it is very easy to follow and it produces the most delicious Sangria at every try :)

The problem however is that the tiny mercado receipt the recipe was originally written on eventually got lost, and with it my Sangria skills! So when the precious piece of paper magically "re-appeared" after going missing for months I decided that it was time to put La Recetta somewhere it can't be lost: HERE on this blog!

So without further ado, here is the recipe for the perfect Sangria, as it was delivered to me:

  • RED WINE
  • FANTA LEMON
  • SUGAR
  • LICORS (GIN, BRANDY)
  • ORANGE LICOR (COINTREAU)
  • FRUITS (LEMON, ORANGE, APPLE)
And that's all there is to know! I personally never tried to add gin but I have added whatever I had on hand at the time (rum, tequila, cherry brandy) and it was awesome too. In fact here is a pic of my first batch of 2014 :)
